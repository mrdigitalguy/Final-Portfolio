The Story Behind the Screens

I’m Kabi Prasad Mallick, a designer fueled by purpose and passion. I believe design has the power to improve lives — and that belief drives everything I create. Each day, I immerse myself in learning something new, staying curious and open to fresh perspectives. I thrive on collaboration and love connecting with bold thinkers who aren't afraid to share their ideas with the world. Over time, I’ve built a strong foundation in UX/UI and product design through hands-on experience, in-depth workshops, and self-driven learning. I see the bigger picture and enjoy shaping products end-to-end — from the first spark of an idea to the final stages of development.

My Values

Passion is Everything

If something doesn’t spark your soul — change it. Life’s too short to stay stuck. I believe in channeling energy into work that feels meaningful, creative, and alive. I’m grateful to be surrounded by people who are driven, curious, and not afraid to dream big. Through design, I want my work to make people feel something — to connect, reflect, and move forward with purpose.

Always Be Learning

I believe growth comes from staying curious and open. Every project, conversation, or challenge is an opportunity to learn something new — whether it's a fresh design approach, a deeper user insight, or a better way to collaborate. I’m constantly exploring new tools, trends, and perspectives to sharpen my craft and expand my thinking. For me, learning isn’t just a phase — it’s a mindset that fuels everything I do.

Embrace Your Creativity

Creativity isn’t just about making things look good — it’s about solving problems in ways that feel fresh, human, and impactful. I believe in giving ideas the freedom to grow, even the messy ones, because that’s where the magic often begins. Whether I’m sketching wireframes or refining final designs, I always try to approach each challenge with curiosity, intention, and an open mind. Creativity, to me, is less about perfection and more about exploration.

Stay Humble

It doesn’t matter if you’re a CEO, a junior designer, or someone still figuring things out — everyone deserves respect. We’re all navigating our own challenges, trying to grow, and looking for meaning in our own way. I believe in treating people with kindness, staying grounded no matter where I go, and never losing sight of who I am. Titles fade, but how you treat others stays with you. Stay real, stay respectful.
